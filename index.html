Welcome to WEB World

The World Wide Web (WWW), commonly known as the Web, is an information system that enables information sharing over
the Internet through user-friendly ways meant to appeal to users beyond IT specialists and hobbyists.
[1] It allows documents and other web resources to be accessed over the Internet according to specific rules of the Hypertext Transfer Protocol(HTTP).[2]
